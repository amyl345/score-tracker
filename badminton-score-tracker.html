How Scoring Works

Badminton Scoring:

  • Games played to 11 or 21 points
  • Must win by 2 points
  • Maximum score is target + 9 points
  • Match is best of 3 sets
  • First to win 2 sets wins the match

Tennis Scoring:

  • Traditional tennis set scoring
  • Games played to 6 games
  • Must win by 2 games or tiebreak at 6-6
info The app automatically advances to the next set when a set is won!
